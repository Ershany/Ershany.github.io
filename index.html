Explore The Lord's Sanguine

Our Games

When the sun fell and the vampire lords claimed the throne, humanity's last hope was forged in blood.

In The Lord's Sanguine, a dark action RPG blending roguelite combat and settlement survival, you are humanity's last bastion against a vampire aristocracy. Build a haven for your people, gather resources, command your armies, and slay the ancient vampire lords who rule from their castles and forests.

Steal their blood through forbidden powers to grow stronger, but every drop pulls you closer to corruption. Will you purify your soul... or embrace the darkness to save what remains?

The Lord's Sanguine is currently in development by Tyrannical Games. Follow for more news and updates!
